Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my Revolut card PIN?

You can reset your card PIN directly in the Revolut app under "Cards" > select your card > "Change PIN".

How to report a lost or stolen card?

Use the Revolut app to instantly freeze your card and request a replacement under the "Cards" section.

What should I do if my transaction is declined?

Ensure you have sufficient funds, check if your card is active, and verify your transaction limits in the app.

How do I contact customer support?

You can chat with us here or reach out via email at support@revolut.com.
